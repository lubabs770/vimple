Welcome. This is lesson one: opening files and saving.

Goal: open src/todo.ts in your editor and change the APP_NAME value from
"CHANGEME" to exactly:
    "vimple-todo"

How (Vim):
  - Run `./vimple edit` to open your Vim at the repo root.
  - Open the file with  :e src/todo.ts
  - Move onto the word CHANGEME and replace it (try {change_in_quotes} on the
    quoted text), type  vimple-todo , then press Esc.
  - Save and quit:  :wq

When done, run  ./vimple check
